Introduction

Zeruleon

Our business is called Zeruleon and we are a local jeweller located here in SLO. We sell every type of jewelery either for a birthday, engagement, or any other type of special occasion. We also buy and fix jewelery here.

Target audience

The target demographic are newlywed couples and young people needing engagement rings and jewelry for family and partners. Most of the people who are to use our website are people who are either trying to view the selection of jewelry that Zeruleon offer or trying to contact one of our esteemed staff for a consultation.

Zeruleon’s primary task and goal revolves around creating a loyal customer base through our high-quality services that we offer (which includes fitting consultations, walk-in appointments, jewelry restoration, and more).

Design sets Zeruleon apart – our number one goal is to make incomparably beautiful engagement rings, wedding rings, gemstone rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, just for you. We carefully consider the entire piece: the artfully tapered prongs, the hidden halo under the center gem, the precisely curved interior of each band. About

At Zeruleon, our mission is to cultivate a more transparent, sustainable, compassionate, and inclusive jewelry industry. We believe in creating jewelry you can feel good about wearing, without ever compromising between quality and conscience. Through responsible sourcing, social impact, and climate action, we strive to raise the ethical standards of the diamond industry and create a new way to buy beautiful fine jewelry.
